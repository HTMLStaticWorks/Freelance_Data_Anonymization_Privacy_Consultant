Our Mission & Vision

Pioneering Privacy Engineering

PrivacyIntel was founded by a team of cybersecurity experts and data scientists dedicated to solving the tension between data utility and individual privacy.

We believe that data is the lifeblood of modern enterprise, but it shouldn't come at the cost of trust. Our mission is to provide the tools and expertise needed to handle data responsibly in a regulated world.
