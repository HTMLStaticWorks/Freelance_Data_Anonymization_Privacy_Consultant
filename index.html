Data Masking Techniques

Pseudonymization

Replacing identifiers with artificial ones to reduce linkability.

ID: #8X9-K2L

Generalization

Reducing granularity of data (e.g., specific age to age ranges).

Age: 25

Perturbation

Adding small amounts of noise to numerical values.

Ensuring each individual is hidden within a group of 'k' others.

The Formula

Minimizing re-identification risk by ensuring quasi-identifiers are shared by at least k individuals.
